Pay traffice fines

This Service allows motorists to inquire about their traffic fines and the Black Points accumulated against them and request to send violation report to the owner's email.

  • All it takes is a couple of minutes to complete. You don't need to enter a lot of personal information to complete this service - all you need to have to hand is:

    • Your vehicle's traffic file number
    • Your vehicle's plate number
    • Your year of birth
    • A credit card to complete payment

  • Secure payment gateway
    We use the Smart Dubai Government gateway which is a highly secure environment for payment. Rest easy in the knowledge that your personal information and credit card details are secure.
